Recipe

Description

Transmitted from China about 700 years ago, “Manju” now has become a representative type of Wagashi or traditional Japanese confection by using a sweet bean paste filling, instead of meat.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Honey
  • Sugar
  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Mirin
  • Vegetable oil
  • Red bean paste

Preparation

  1. First, you will have to make the batter and refrigerate it for 30 minutes.
  2. Then, fry the batter on a pan as you’d typically do to make pancakes.
  3. Lastly, Spread your preferred filling on one cake and sandwich it with another cake.
  4. Enjoy!
